Why do Bronxdale families choose Khan's?

No matter what grade your child is in, Khan's will establish all
fundamental Common Core standards and provide a unique education for each student's needs.

3rd Grade

For students taking standardized tests for the first time, the experience can be uncomfortable. We provide our 3rd grade students with extra practice through our Signature Diagnostic exams to ensure that they'll be comfortable when testing day comes around.

4th Grade

The Common Core exam determines which Middle school your child is accepted into, therefore, we ensure your child completely understands all Common Core topics. Additionally, the NYS Science exam is administered in 4th grade so we cover all science curriculum as well.

5th Grade

The 5th Grade Common Core program covers all aspects of the Common Core curriculum and prepares students for Middle school admissions. Students with perfect scores (4/4) qualify for the Hunter College High School exam.

6th Grade

Starting middle school can be a challenging time for students. The KT Common Core program for 6th graders will assist with the transition from elementary school to middle school to ensure a successful middle school experience.

7th Grade

The 7th grade exam is essential to the High School admissions process and we will ensure your child is prepared for these exams. In addition, our program provides Pre-SHSAT education, giving students a head start with a strong foundation. You can learn more about the SHSAT here

Our Curriculum

Developed by Senior Common Core leaders at KT, our curriculum
covers all aspects of the Common Core Curriculum and beyond.

Establishing Fundamentals

We will work with your child to establish basic fundamentals to ensure a strong foundation for further learning.

Common Core Curriculum

Your child's KT Instructor will cover the Common Core curriculum for ELA, Math & Writing as well as any Science or Social Studies material from school.

Middle School Admissions

KT prepares 4th and 5th grade students for Middle School Admissions and assists in the transition to Middle School for 6th grade students.

And Beyond

We provide Hunter College High School preperation for those that need it and work with students to build a strong foundation for the SHSAT.

Our Program

We prepare our students to master the NYS Common Core curriculum
to ultimately do well on their Citywide and Statewide examinations.

Weekend Sessions

Report Cards & Parent-Teacher Conferences

Comprehensive report cards will be completed by your child's Instructor every month so you can track your child's progress. We also hold mandatory Parent-Teacher Conferences to ensure you're involved in your child's learning process.
